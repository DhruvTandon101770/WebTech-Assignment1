A Legacy of Liberation

Shrimati Meera Kapoor's story serves as the cornerstone of Meeraki Prints.
A woman of the late 1950s, she pursued higher education, excelled in classical Bharatnatyam dance, and passionately advocated for women's rights through her writing.
Meeraki Prints, a tribute to her love for writing, doodling, and preserving memories, started as a humble collection of notebooks, offering blank canvases for self-ex-pression.
